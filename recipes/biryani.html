The Ulimate Biryani Recipe

Here's a detailed recipe for biryani:

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken or lamb, cut into pieces
  • 3 cups basmati rice, washed and soaked in water for 30 minutes
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chillies, slit
  • 1/2 cup yoghurt
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • Oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat oil and ghee. Add the bay leaves, cloves, green cardamoms, and cinnamon stick. Fry for a few seconds until fragrant.
  2. Add the sliced onions and fry until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add the ginger-garlic paste and green chillies. Fry for a few seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes and fry until they turn mushy.
  5. Add the chicken or lamb pieces and fry until they turn golden brown.
  6. Add the turmeric powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and garam masala powder. Mix well.
  7. Add the yoghurt and salt. Mix well and cook until the meat is cooked through and tender.
  8. In a separate pot, boil enough water to cook the soaked rice. Add the soaked rice and some salt. Cook until the rice is about 80% done. Drain the water and set aside.
  9. In a large pot, layer the rice and meat mixture. Start with a layer of rice, followed by a layer of meat mixture, and then another layer of rice. Repeat until all the rice and meat mixture has been used up.
  10. Add a few spoons of ghee on top of the final layer of rice. Cover the pot with a lid and place it on a low flame. Cook for 10-15 minutes until the rice is fully cooked and fluffy.

Now Enjoy!
